Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 Dec 2025

What I collect

When you contact me through the website, I may collect your name, email address, phone number (if provided), location (if provided), and the message you submit.

Why I collect it

I use this information to respond to your enquiry, provide support, and keep basic records for administration.

How your data is handled

Website enquiries are sent to me by email and may be processed by my form provider (Formspree) to deliver the message. I do not sell your data or share it with third parties for marketing.

How long I keep it

I keep enquiry messages only for as long as needed to respond and for reasonable business/admin purposes.

Your rights

You can ask to access or delete your information at any time by emailing info@euanscomputersupport.co.uk.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, email info@euanscomputersupport.co.uk.
